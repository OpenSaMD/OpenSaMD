Failure Mode and Effects Analysis (FMEA): Risk Acceptance Matrix#

This document defines under which circumstances risk are acceptable. It defines probabilities and severities and each probability-severity combination is either acceptable or not, as shown in the risk acceptance matrix at the bottom.

Risk Policy#

Radiotherapy AI’s products should not cause more harm than undergoing the process manually without AI assistance.

Severity#

Severity

Definition and Examples

S1: Negligible

Delay of treatment or clinically non-relevant protocol deviation

S2: Minor

Minor radiotherapy protocol deviation, non-patient identifying data leak

S3: Major

Major radiotherapy protocol deviation, or patient identifying data leak

S4: Critical

Reportable radiotherapy mistreatment and/or incident

S5: Death
